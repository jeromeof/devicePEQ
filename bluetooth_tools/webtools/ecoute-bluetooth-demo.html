ℹ️ How to use:
1. Make sure your Ecoute TH1 headphones are powered on
2. Click "Connect to Ecoute" to filter
3. Select "écoute" from the browser's device picker - make sure to pick the one that is paired
4. Use "Query Current EQ" to read current EQ settings from device
5. Adjust sliders manually or use a preset
6. Click "Apply EQ Profile" to write changes to device

⚠️ IMPORTANT: While the EQ is saved to the Ecoute device, if you open the Ecoute app and view or change anything (even just viewing the Frequency page), the app will restore the EQ to what it had stored previously. Keep this in mind when testing!
